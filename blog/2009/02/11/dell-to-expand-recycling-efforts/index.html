Dell to Expand Recycling Efforts

Feb 11th, 2009

Dell is expanding its recycling efforts by adding six additional states to its drop off at GoodWill program and an online exchange program. Reconnectpartnership.com has a list of dropoff locations.

More information is available in Dell to Pay a post by Jonathan Hiskes.
